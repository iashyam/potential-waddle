  \
I stand on the hill \
Watching the sun disappear\
Into the darkness. \
  \
I wait for the moon\
To rise from the horizon\
And end the darkness. \
  \
But light doesn't come \
And dark clouds take over the sky\
Black the conqueror! \
  \
The hill sounds like \
Silent cries of dying hope\
And the light never comes.\
  \
  \
