title: Diamond Duck

Once, I got diamond duck\
It was my trun\
Need two runs\
I took the bat\
I drove straight\
For run I ran\
Ball found man\
'Oh... Shit!' \
Direct hit\
I hit my head\
No ball was said\
I cried WTF! \
I got diamond duck.\
  \
~Shyam Sunder
